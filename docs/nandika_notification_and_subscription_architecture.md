💡 Universal Notification Rule

Every notification must answer:

✅ What happened?

✅ Why is it important?

✅ Who is affected?

✅ What should the user do?

✅ What is the deadline?

✅ What is the official source?

✅ Where can the user learn more?

🌟 Nandika Notification Philosophy
